Weekly Review Rules

If weight is dropping >2 lb/week:Keep training intensity but watch recovery and protein.
If weight is flat 10+ days:Add 10 min Zone 2 after two sessions or tighten weekend calories.
If strength drops 2 workouts in a row:Reduce sprint volume by 20% for one week.
If abs are the focus:Do core with control. No sloppy reps just to feel burn.
